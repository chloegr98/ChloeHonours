Invocation Name: Further Notes
Additionally, when you are thinking off and making an Invocation Name for your Alexa Skill there are a few requirements and details that should be taken into consideration. These are:
- Invocation Names should be two words or more
- Numbers should be written out, for example - "twenty one" for 21
- You are allowed to use spaces in between words.
- It's ok to use apostrophes, but only if they are used possessively.
Use a full stop when you are abbreviating something.
You should be using lowercase words
