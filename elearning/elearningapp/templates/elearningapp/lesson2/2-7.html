Utterances

These are the different ways in which someone could say their message to Alexa, not everyone will say the exact same message. Utterances are derivatives of the original request - said differently. Sample utterances should be set in order to improve the accuracy of the Alexa Skill and be able to give users who say the request in a different way the same response. For example,

Some users may say: “Alexa, ask AlarmApp to set an alarm for 4am.”

While other users may just say: “4am AlarmApp”
